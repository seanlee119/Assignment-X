Name

Will it happen?

Description:

It is easy to tell a story when it already happened. The future though is always unknown. In this assignment you will be creating a game in which the storytelling is based around prediciting the future.

Assignment Purpose

The purpose of this assignment is to get the student to understand analysis. When telling stories a lot of readers like to predict things on what is going to happen next. Analyzing is crucial for any reader or author. As the reader you want to pick up clues to try and guess what will happen. As the author you don't want to make the story too predictable or the story will get boring. Understanding how important analysis will allow major imporvements. By creating a game that revolves around the unknown it forces the student to learn how the players can analyze a given situation and use that to predict what will happen. Thinking ahead is a very crucial skill to have in a multitude of things whether that be in games or living your daily life.

Assignment Specification:

These are the requirements for the game.

  1. The game must have a narrative that revolves around trying to predict something by analyzing a given situtation
  2. The game can be in any shape or form but must be able to be presented online.
  3. The game should take around 10-20 minutes to play.

You must also have people play this game and write a paragraph on a pdf on how the player/players tried to predict, analyze, and think ahead. Have the game and the pdf zipped up together and turn in that zip file.

Collaboration:

For every person in the group have an additional layer of analysis required to get to the goal. The more people, the more complicated and harder the game should be and it should also take longer.
